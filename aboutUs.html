About our NGO


We train young people into communication & multimedia, we give media support to events that sustain a good cause & we empower youth to in their projects!


Prayaas is a non-governmental, non-profit association founded in 2008 with the aim of contributing to community sustainable development by means of education and active involvement of young people into cross-sectorial fields of activity in our ‘global village’. We cultivate media literacy among youth at local, regional, international level and use multimedia and digital technology as drivers for education and change. Mostly we are training young people from various backgrounds to use multimedia instruments and digital softwares for editing, design and post-production in order to empower them to express creatively proving social integrity. The topics we address in our projects are socially emerged and point the view of a cultural, ecological and social healthy environment: human rights, cultural and bio diversity, anti discrimination, involvement of disadvantaged, free education, right information.

Our Values


MISSION: We promote free education, media literacy & the right to true information, we enhance young people's communication and multimedia skills, we take real actions in sustainable directions.
